Vision of Arunoday Foundation is to provide quality education to the underprivileged rural children of India by developing a sustainable model and replicating it across many villages using modern technologies to the extent possible with the co-operation of like-minded individuals and institutions.

Mission of Arunoday Foundation is to put into practice Tapovani Mata Sant Subhadra’s appeal to spread “Shiksha, Swastya, Sanskar” to the rural masses by opening and managing high quality educational institutions in rural areas.

About Us

Arunoday Foundation was formed/registered on 25th of Feb 2016. The mission of the foundation is to provide quality education and healthcare to the rural children. The inspiration comes from Tapovani Mata Sant Subhadra’s drive to spread ‘Shiksha, Swasthya and Sanskar’. The founder Narendra Shetty and General Secretary Gajendra Singh have been followers of Tapovani Mata for a long time.A beginning has been made taking a tough challenge. A pre-primary school has been opened at a remote mountain village called Juguldi in Uttarkashi, with no transport or other amenities, 5 km away from nearest tar road. Medical check-up of the school children and their parents will be done at regular intervals and a record will be maintained.
