Our NGO School is on a mission to provide quality education to the children residing in the rural areas. This initiative will help rural children to get english education in their formative years and explore the vast knowledge pool. One might wonder why english language is given so much more importance than our mother tongue. Well there is a perfect reason, english language is the most common second language worldwide. About 420 million people across the globe in about 53 countries speaks english language. Even most of the MNC’s have mandated english language as their official corporate language. So just by learning a language can make a difference in a child's life.

School Infrastructure

Our NGO school has a building with 4 classrooms with a chalkboard, many charts and many posters which provides a perfect environment to study. Our school members have a separate office.There are 2 toilets in the school and a small playground for outside activities.

Medical Facilities

Medical check-up of the school children and their parents will be done at regular intervals and a record has been maintained. Healthcare and hygiene education will be provided to parents. .

Computer Lab

A computer lab provided by Mr Dheeraj Kaushik, to familiarize the children and download study materials and view study material online. Students can perform different experiments such as learning excel, PowerPoint and paint in our computer lab.
